Stripe
Send Invoice & Collect Payment
Create and send a Stripe invoice before any domain work or file transfer. Client pays by card or ACH bank transfer. Payment must clear before handoff begins.
Pricing reminders
  • Standard build: $3,500 — position as outcome, not hours
  • Default to ACH: 0.8% capped at $5 vs 2.9% for card
  • ACH on $3,500 = $5 fee · Card on $3,500 = ~$102 fee
  • Never transfer files or domain before payment clears
Stripe account

Make
Auto-Log Payment + Tax Alert
Fires the moment Stripe confirms payment. Logs the transaction in Notion, calculates the 30% tax set-aside, and sends you a notification with the exact dollar amount to move.
Tax formula (30% — TX, federal only)
net = amount − stripe_fee
set_aside = net × 0.30
keep = net × 0.70
Make scenario steps
  • Trigger: Stripe → payment_intent.succeeded
  • Parse: amount ÷ 100 (Stripe stores in cents)
  • Calculate: net, set_aside, keep (formula above)
  • Action 1: Notion → Create Transaction row (Income)
  • Action 2: Notion → Create Transaction row (Stripe Fee, Expense)
  • Action 3: Email → send tax set-aside alert (see below)
  • Action 4: Notion → Create Tax Reminder row with checkbox
